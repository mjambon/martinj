Ecru #6
Martin J., June 2025
46 cm × 61 cm (18 in × 24 in), acrylic and brass leaf on 12-mm maple plywood
Holism isn't the refusal to recognize that a system is made up of parts, it's the acknowledgment that modifying one part affects the perception of the other parts.
This has been a major driver behind my art. It leads me to dropping many traditional guidelines when it comes to composition and color—as well as to adopting new ones. – MJ
